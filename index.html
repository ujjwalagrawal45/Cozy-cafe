Our Story

Founded in 2026, Cozy Cafe brings the nostalgic taste of India to you. We source the finest tea leaves from Assam and spices from Kerala. Our space is designed to be your home away from home—a place to relax, work, or catch up with friends over a steaming kulhad of chai.
